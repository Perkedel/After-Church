# How to play
## Controls: 
### Camera
- Swipe surrounding (off the character) = rotate camera around her
- Pinch or Panch surrounding (off the character) = zoom in or out
- 2x tap crotch = lower the camera
- 2x tap face = raise the camera
### Steth
- Hold which area of chest to be stethed at = Put stethoscope head towards chest onto your finger position. You can release once the head successfully placed.
### Zoom situations
- Far = lets her do her action & show the scene selector on top left
- Close to chest = enables ear steth button.
## Toolbar
- Xray ON/OFF = (Exam scene only) switch between Xray or normal camera mode on the Xray gun. You will see the view on the monitor behind her.
- Heart Rate Hide/Show = Show or hide heart rate monitor. there is animated icon showing heart beating status & its rate number in BPM.
- Steth On/Off = Draw or undraw stethoscope. Drawing the stethoscope switches the Audio Listener / Microphone position to the stethoscope. You can then put the stethoscope head on her chest.
- Breathhold = Command her to do breathhold. Breathholding will cause her heart to increase in rate, as it wants the oxygen. She can only breathhold for limited amount of time.
- Action = Command her to do the action. Note that you need to zoom away from her chest to give her motion space.
- Ear Steth = (only when zoomed in close to chest) Put your head (your camera view) to her chest & listen to her heart. You will always listen with left side currently. Your camera head will stick to it & **Mega Visible Heartbeat** goes with it.
