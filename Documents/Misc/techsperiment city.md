# Techsperiment city, Dasandim

A city where a novelty tech put to the daily life. E.g., wall outlets are USB-C, and has all the data and power. The internet router is also included in the battery bank package, citizens only has to pay one bill for power & internet.

Although most citizen also have their own power generator & solo internet for $0, they only pay for in case backup recharging and backup public v4 legacy IP address.
