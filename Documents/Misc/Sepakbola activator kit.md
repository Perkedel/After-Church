# Sepakbola Activator kit

Free DLC mod for Sport Kit in Hexagon Engine.

## Description

Indonesia has been one of if not the most neglected country on Earth. Especially in Soccer world, FIFA as the master of the Soccer world neglects and also punishes Indonesia among others both other nations & every aspects possible tremendous amount of times. This builds sense that Indonesia is never worth the value and those who born on Indonesia deserves desertion to another better nations.

To respect this unfortunate situation, Perkedel decided to not include or otherwise enable the Soccer feature out of the box. User shall download the separate DLC which available for free from our mod page here on LBRY. Until the situation gets better, for now, the soccer mode & other goal tackle related modes (except Tchoukball) are not included out of the box.
